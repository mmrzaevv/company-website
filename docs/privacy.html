Privacy Policy

GlobePro Transport LLC ("GlobePro", "we", "our", or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our website, forms, email, phone, and SMS communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name

  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Dispatch, shipment, or service-related details you submit to us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Provide SMS communications when you opt in

3. SMS Messaging Privacy

If you provide your mobile phone number through a website form or other direct communication channel and expressly consent to receive text messages, GlobePro Transport LLC may send you SMS messages related to:

  • Dispatch updates
  • Load offers
  • Shipment status updates
  • Driver coordination
  • Customer support responses
  • Account or service notifications

Message frequency may vary.

Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Reply HELP for assistance.

4. Consent for SMS Communications

By submitting your phone number through our website form and checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive SMS messages from GlobePro Transport LLC related to transportation, dispatch, logistics, and customer support communications.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us using the information below.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including mobile opt-in data or consent, with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only when necessary to operate our business, comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or provide requested services.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or alteration.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Your Choices

You may:

  • Request that we update or correct your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
