Privacy Policy

How GlobePro Transport LLC collects, uses, and protects your information

Privacy Policy

GlobePro Transport LLC respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our website, forms, email, phone, and SMS communications.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal and business information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Dispatch, shipment, service, or support-related details you submit to us

How We Use Information

We use collected information only for legitimate business and service purposes, including:

  • Responding to transportation or logistics inquiries
  • Providing freight, dispatch, and operational support
  • Coordinating with drivers, customers, and business partners
  • Sending account, service, shipment, or dispatch updates
  • Providing SMS communications when you have given consent

SMS Messaging Privacy

If you provide your mobile phone number through our website form and expressly agree to receive text messages, GlobePro Transport LLC may send SMS communications related to:

  • Dispatch updates
  • Load offers
  • Shipment status updates
  • Driver coordination
  • Customer support responses
  • Service or account notifications

Message frequency may vary.

Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Reply HELP for assistance.

SMS Consent

By submitting your phone number through our website form and checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive SMS messages from GlobePro Transport LLC related to transportation services, dispatch communication, logistics coordination, shipment updates, customer support, and service notifications.

SMS consent is not a condition of purchase. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us directly.

Mobile opt-in information and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

GlobePro Transport LLC does not sell, rent, or distribute personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only when necessary to provide requested services, operate our business, comply with legal obligations, or protect our legal rights.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or alteration.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce business agreements.

Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Request updates or corrections to your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Contact us with questions regarding your privacy or data usage
