Privacy Policy

How Globepro Transport LLC collects and uses information

1. Information We Collect

Globepro Transport LLC may collect personal information when you contact us through our website, email, or phone.

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company information
  • Transportation service inquiries

2. How We Use Information

The information collected may be used to:

  • Respond to service inquiries
  • Provide transportation and logistics services
  • Communicate regarding shipments or driver coordination
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service-related notifications

3. SMS Communication

If you provide your phone number and consent to receive text messages, Globepro Transport LLC may send SMS messages related to:

  • Shipment updates
  • Driver communication
  • Service notifications
  • Customer support responses

Message frequency may vary depending on service activity. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

You may opt out of SMS communication at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message.

4. Information Sharing

Globepro Transport LLC does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may be shared with trusted partners only when necessary to provide logistics or transportation services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Globepro Transport LLC.

Email: dispatch@globeprotransport.com
